Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

📅 Effective date: March 30, 2026

1 Overview

Quick Close CRM ("we", "us", or "our") provides an AI-powered sales assistant that connects to Instagram business accounts to automate responses to incoming direct messages and track sales leads.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how it is used, and how you can contact us with questions or requests.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

2 Data We Collect

When an Instagram user sends a message to a business account connected to Quick Close CRM, we may collect:

  • Instagram username of the person sending the message
  • Instagram user ID (a numeric identifier assigned by Meta)
  • The text content of direct messages sent to the connected business account
  • Images or media attachments sent as payment vouchers or proof of purchase
  • Message timestamps and conversation thread identifiers

We do not collect passwords, financial account numbers, government IDs, or any data beyond what is delivered through Instagram's Messaging API.

3 How We Use Your Data

Data collected is used exclusively to provide the service. Specifically:

  • Generate automated AI sales responses on behalf of the connected business
  • Track lead status (active, won, lost) and conversation temperature for the business owner's CRM dashboard
  • Store conversation history so the AI can maintain context across messages
  • Detect payment vouchers to mark a sale as completed
  • Display lead analytics to the business that owns the Instagram account
Automated responses are generated by an AI model. Business owners can review all conversations in their dashboard at any time.

4 Data Retention

Conversation data (messages, lead records, and voucher images) is retained for as long as the business account remains active on Quick Close CRM.

If a business account is deleted or cancelled, associated lead and message data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

Instagram users may request deletion of their data at any time by contacting us at the email below. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

5 Data Sharing

We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With the business you messaged — they can see conversations and lead status on their dashboard
  • Anthropic — message content is sent to the Claude AI API to generate responses, subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy
  • Supabase — data is stored in Supabase-hosted PostgreSQL databases, subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy
  • Legal requirements — if required by law or to protect our legal rights

6 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Objection to or restriction of processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the email address below. We will respond within 30 days.

7 Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), row-level security on our database so each business can only access their own data, and API key authentication on all endpoints.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. If you believe your data has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

8 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
